# My Journey

On July 22, 2026 I got rejected from Google FDE.

I made it to the final round. The RRK round went well. The DSA round was the gap. There is a 12-month cooling off period before I can reapply.

---

## What Happened

I prepared. Not well enough.

The DSA round exposed what I already knew but hadn't fully addressed: pattern recognition under time pressure, with the added complexity of FDE-style real-world framing. I went silent when I should have talked. I started coding before I'd fully understood the problem.

The RRK went better — I know GCP, I know AI systems. That part felt like a real conversation.

The gap was the DSA round. And the DSA round is fixable.
