## Why These Certifications

**Anthropic certs:** Two reasons. Anthropic is a target company. And genuinely deep understanding of the Claude API and prompt engineering is rare and valuable in any AI role.

**GCP cert track:** Google FDE is the goal. Having the Professional Cloud Architect certification tells a Google interviewer that you've studied their platform systematically enough to pass their own exam. It's not sufficient, but it's a meaningful signal.

**Why in this order:** Start where the bar is lower (Cloud Digital Leader), build knowledge, move to the harder and more valuable certs. Don't skip the foundation certs — they teach you things the harder ones assume.
