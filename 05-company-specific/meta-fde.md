# Meta FDE Interview Guide

## Role Overview

Meta's FDE role sits within the Business Engineering organization. FDEs work with Meta's largest advertising partners, commerce customers, and enterprise API users.

**Core function:** Help Meta's strategic business partners succeed with Meta's API products — Ads API, Commerce API, WhatsApp Business API, Instagram Graph API.

**AI shift (2024-2026):** Meta has aggressively pushed Llama, PyTorch, and AI products. FDE roles now require meaningful AI depth — Llama 3/4 ecosystem, Meta AI Studio, and AI ads optimization.

---

## Interview Structure

**Coding round:** Standard LeetCode-style DSA. Medium to hard.

**System design:** Often advertising-adjacent. "Design an ad targeting system," "Design a click attribution pipeline."

**Behavioral:** Meta uses "values" rather than formal leadership principles. The key values: Move Fast, Be Bold, Focus on Long-Term Impact, Be Open, Build Social Value.

---

## Technical Areas to Know

**Advertising infrastructure:**
- How digital advertising works (impressions, clicks, conversions, attribution)
- Conversion API — server-side event tracking
- Meta Pixel — browser-side tracking
- Attribution windows and models

**Meta AI:**
- Llama 3/4 — Meta's open-source LLM family
- PyTorch — Meta's ML framework (know the basics)
- Meta AI Studio — build AI with Llama
- Advantage+ (AI-powered ad campaigns) — understand the concept

**Scale:**
- Meta operates at a scale few companies match
- Think in billions of events per day
- Consistency vs. availability tradeoffs at this scale

---

## Preparation Tips

- Build something with the Meta Marketing API or WhatsApp Business API — hands-on experience stands out
- Understand how programmatic advertising works end-to-end
- Know Llama — download it, run it locally, understand how it compares to GPT and Claude
- Behavioral stories should emphasize impact at scale and moving fast
