# Amazon FDE / Technical Account Manager Guide

## Role Overview

Amazon's customer-facing technical roles include:

- **Technical Account Manager (TAM)** — enterprise support, strategic guidance
- **Solutions Architect (SA)** — pre-sales technical architecture
- **Customer Engineer / Specialist SA** — deep technical implementation
- **AI/ML Specialist SA** — dedicated AI/ML customer-facing role

For FDE-background engineers, the AI/ML Specialist SA and Senior Solutions Architect roles are the closest match.

---

## The Leadership Principles — Non-Negotiable

Amazon's Leadership Principles are the most structured behavioral framework in the industry. Every single behavioral answer must map to one or more LPs.

**The 16 LPs you must know:**
1. Customer Obsession
2. Ownership
3. Invent and Simplify
4. Are Right, A Lot
5. Learn and Be Curious
6. Hire and Develop the Best
7. Insist on the Highest Standards
8. Think Big
9. Bias for Action
10. Frugality
11. Earn Trust
12. Dive Deep
13. Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit
14. Deliver Results
15. Strive to be Earth's Best Employer
16. Success and Scale Bring Broad Responsibility

**For FDE-equivalent roles, focus on:** Customer Obsession, Ownership, Dive Deep, Bias for Action, Deliver Results, Invent and Simplify.

## Interview Structure

**Phone screen:** LP-focused behavioral questions

**Onsite (loop, usually 5-6 rounds):**
- 4-5 behavioral/LP rounds with different interviewers
- 1-2 technical rounds (system design, coding at some levels)
- Writing exercise at some levels (Amazon values writing)

**Bar raiser:** One interviewer is a designated bar raiser — their vote carries extra weight. They look for "raise the bar" quality, not just "good enough."

## AWS Technical Areas

For AI/ML roles:
- **SageMaker** — model training, deployment, pipelines
- **Bedrock** — foundation models as a service (Claude, Llama, Titan)
- **SageMaker Jumpstart** — pre-built ML solutions
- **OpenSearch Service** — vector search for RAG

General AWS depth:
- VPC, IAM, S3, Lambda, EC2, RDS, DynamoDB, Kinesis, SQS/SNS

## Behavioral Preparation

For each LP, prepare 2-3 STAR stories. Use the **STAR + "Raise the Bar"** formula:

Standard: Situation, Task, Action, Result  
Amazon bonus: Why was your approach better than the obvious solution? What did you do that others wouldn't have?

Amazon interviewers specifically look for: data-driven decisions, frugality, customer outcomes, and examples where you disagreed but committed.
