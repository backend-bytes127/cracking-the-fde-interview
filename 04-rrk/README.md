# Role Related Knowledge (RRK)

The RRK round is what separates FDE preparation from SWE preparation.

RRK tests your depth in the specific technical domain that FDE work lives in. At Google FDE, that's primarily GCP architecture and AI/ML systems. At Anthropic, it's LLMs, Claude API, and AI evaluation. At Meta, it's the advertising and Llama ecosystem.

This section covers the RRK topics for Google FDE specifically — the most structured and testable of the FDE RRK rounds.

## The RRK Mental Model

In a real FDE role, your job is to walk into a customer's architecture meeting and contribute immediately. The RRK round tests whether you could do that.

The interviewer is asking: *"If a Fortune 500 customer's CTO asked this person about deploying a RAG system on GCP tomorrow, would they have a coherent answer?"*

Prepare to answer:
1. "How would you architect X on GCP?"
2. "A customer is having problem Y — what do you do?"
3. "What are the tradeoffs between approach A and approach B?"
4. "Tell me about a time you solved a technical problem for a customer."
